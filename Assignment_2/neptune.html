8. Neptune

Neptune is a cold, windy, and dark planet that's the farthest planet from the sun in our solar system. It's known as an ice giant because it's made of a thick fog of water, ammonia, and methane over a solid core. Neptune is the only planet in our solar system not visible to the naked eye.
