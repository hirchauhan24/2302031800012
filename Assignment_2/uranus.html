7. Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun and the third largest planet in our solar system. It’s an ice giant planet with a thick atmosphere, 13 rings, and more than two dozen moons. It is a gaseous cyan-colored ice giant. Uranus is the only planet that spins on its side.
