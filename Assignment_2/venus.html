2. Venus

Venus is a rocky planet with a thick atmosphere, volcanoes, and a surface temperature of 464°C. It's often called Earth's twin because it's similar in size and density. Venus is the third brightest object in the sky after the Sun and Moon. Venus spins slowly in the opposite direction from most planets.

Diameter 7,520.8 Miles
Day time Temperature 460° Celsius
Night time Temperature 460° Celsius
