9. PLUTO

Pluto is a dwarf planet in the Kuiper Belt, a distant region of our solar system. It was once considered the ninth planet but was reclassified in 2006 by the International Astronomical Union (IAU). Pluto is about 1/6th the size of Earth and is smaller than Earth's moon.
